Patient Information

At We Care Wellness Clinic, we’re here to make your experience smooth, comfortable, and stress-free.

This page provides everything you need to know before, during, and after your visit.

Before Your Visit

Book your appointment in advance to minimize waiting time.

Bring your previous prescriptions and medical records.

Carry recent investigation and lab reports, if available.

Arrive a few minutes early to complete any formalities.

2

What to Bring

Valid Photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)

Previous medical reports and test results

List of current medicines with doses

List of questions or concerns you want to discuss

3

During the Consultation

Detailed discussion of your medical history and symptoms

Thorough clinical evaluation and necessary examination

Discussion of test results and possible diagnosis

Personalized treatment plan and health guidance

4

Follow-up & Ongoing Care

Attend follow-up visits as advised for the best outcomes

Monitor your condition and share any changes

Adhere to prescribed treatment and lifestyle advice

Focus on preventive care to maintain long-term wellness

Frequently Asked Questions

You can book an appointment by clicking the "Book Appointment" button on this page, calling the clinic directly, or visiting us in person during our working hours.

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking an appointment in advance to minimize your waiting time, especially during peak hours.

We manage a wide range of conditions including general medicine concerns, diabetes, hypertension, respiratory and allergy issues, infections, and preventive health check-ups for the whole family.
